Community Support
=================

Get help from the Neo4j open source community, here are some starting points:

* http://neo4j.org/forums/[Forum / Mailing list (searchable)], http://www.mail-archive.com/user@lists.neo4j.org/info.html[archive], http://neo4j.org/#SubscribeForm[sign up].
* http://help.neo4j.org/[Neo4j Support] with http://help.neo4j.org/discussions[discussions] and a http://help.neo4j.org/kb[knowledge base].
* IRC channel: irc://irc.freenode.net/neo4j http://webchat.freenode.net/?randomnick=1&channels=neo4j[web chat].
* http://wiki.neo4j.org/[Neo4j wiki].
